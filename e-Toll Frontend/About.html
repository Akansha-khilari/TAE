About E-Toll System

Welcome to our E-Toll System, a streamlined solution for easy toll payments and vehicle management. Developed as part of the TAE project at G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering, this one-page app offers an intuitive interface for user registration, vehicle registration, and hassle-free toll payments.

Our system ensures that drivers can swiftly and securely register themselves and their vehicles, keeping track of toll payments with just a few clicks. Save time and avoid manual toll hassles with our state-of-the-art E-Toll solution.
